Capabilities

Everything a SOC analyst needs, nothing they don't.

From raw pcap to structured, MITRE-mapped evidence in under a second — in the browser, on your machine.

100% In-Browser Analysis
Rust compiled to WASM runs entirely on your CPU. No server, no cloud, no account. Works air-gapped. Your captures are private by design.
20+ Threat Detectors
Behavioral and signature detection: port scans, C2 beaconing, DGA, ARP spoofing, SYN floods, lateral movement, exfiltration, cryptomining, cleartext credentials — each mapped to MITRE ATT&CK.
Deep Protocol Intelligence
TLS fingerprinting (JA3/JA4/JA3S/HASSH), SNI from TLS and QUIC/HTTP3, passive DNS, HTTP metadata, DoH/DoT visibility, and file carving with SHA-256 IOC matching.
One-Click Triage Dashboard
Severity-ranked findings, a virtualized flows grid handling millions of rows, a threat relationship graph, protocol sunburst, and score waterfall — all rendered in the browser.
AI Analyst (Privacy-Preserving)
Optional natural-language executive summary and chat. Sends only a derived statistical summary to the model — raw packets never leave your device. Requires explicit consent.
Open & Interoperable
Export STIX 2.1, MISP events, CEF, Sigma rules, and CSV. Import Suricata rules and apply them in-app. Generate standalone HTML reports that share without dependencies.

Privacy by architecture
Your packets never leave your device

Most network analysis tools require you to upload capture files to a cloud server. PacketPilot is built differently: the entire analysis engine is compiled to WebAssembly and runs inside your browser tab. Your captures stay exactly where they are — on your disk.
