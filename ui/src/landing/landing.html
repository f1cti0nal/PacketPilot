The Platform

Everything a SOC analyst needs,
in one private workspace.

Severity-ranked triage from one drop
Drop a capture and get findings ranked critical to info in seconds — each with the evidence behind it and a transparent risk score.
20+ MITRE-mapped detectors
Behavioral and signature detection: C2 beaconing, DGA, port scans, ARP spoofing, SYN floods, lateral movement, exfiltration, cryptomining, cleartext creds — each mapped to ATT&CK.
Deep protocol & TLS intelligence
JA3/JA4/JA3S/HASSH fingerprints, SNI from TLS and QUIC/HTTP3, passive DNS, HTTP metadata, and file carving with SHA-256 IOC matching.
Open exports + an AI analyst
Export STIX 2.1, MISP, CEF, Sigma, CSV, and standalone HTML reports. An optional, privacy-preserving AI summary sends only derived stats — never packets.

How it works

From capture to evidence
in three steps.

No setup, no upload, no account — just drop a file.

01
Drop a .pcap
Drag-and-drop a .pcap, .pcapng, or .gz file. Works in any modern browser. Nothing is uploaded.
.pcap · .pcapng · .pcap.gz
02
Instant triage
The Rust/WASM engine decodes packets, runs 20+ detectors, fingerprints TLS flows, and ranks findings by severity — in seconds, right in your browser.

Export evidence
Download STIX, MISP, Sigma, CEF, or CSV. Share a self-contained HTML report. Re-apply Suricata rules on the next run.
